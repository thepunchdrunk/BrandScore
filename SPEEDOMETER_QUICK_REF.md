## FAQ

**Q: Why does my score keep changing?**
A: Each analysis evaluates fresh content. Edit text, re-analyze, score updates.

**Q: What's the difference between component scores?**
A: Different aspects of brand alignment. Low one score might need specific attention.

**Q: How do I improve my score?**
A: Fix flagged issues shown below. Use suggested edits. Re-analyze to see improvement.

**Q: Can I ignore red flags?**
A: Not recommended. Red zone content requires escalation and likely major changes.

**Q: Does animation speed matter?**
A: No - 1.2 second animation is just for visual feedback. Actual analysis is instant.

**Q: Works offline?**
A: Yes! All processing happens in your browser. No data sent anywhere.
